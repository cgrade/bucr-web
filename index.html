Bucr is the No. 1 platform in Nigeria
that help you discover and book the perfect Restaurant, Bar and lounge for your occasion!

Features

  1. Search for Restaurant

    2. You Can search for a specific restaurant, by searching for the name of the restaurant,

    When you get a restaurant you like, you can view the restaurant's profile, see their opening hours,

    menu, and interior feel and then you can finally book a table for your occassion

  1. Create Reservation

    2. after searching for a restaurant of choice, you can book reservation

    when creating reservation you need to set the Date, Time, Number of Guests and table number

  1. View your Reservations

    2. You can view your reservation history and can cancel any reservation

About

My name is Abraham Elijah, and I work as a Chef at The Zuma Grill Restaurant, Transcorp Hilton Hotel ABuja FCT. I have been working there for over 5 years, and i noticed that there isn't a platform where our diner's could actually book for a reservation online before coming to the restaurant. i made couple of research, found out that there's nothing of such in Nigeria or even Africa as a whole. that was how the Idea for Bucr was inspired. This is a Portfolio Project for ALX Software Engineering, but i'm taking it further to become a Startup as the application has a very large market to generate profits for Restaurant owners and founders alike.
